From Our DYD:

"...The Lord your God will always be with you." Joshua 1:9.   2014 was an amazing year for CDDC youth department.   We are trusting in the Lord for a greater year in 2015.  Our vision as a youth department is for every church to have a healthy, thriving youth group in 2015. Please let me know if I can assist you and your youth leader in any way. 

The Lord is awesome!  Convention 2014 exceeded our expectations.  The floodgates of Heaven were open as the Holy Spirit submerged us with His presence among the people in attendance.  The youth came ready to receive from the Lord and left convention to answer the ”Call To Duty".  We know our youth are on a mission to leave a Legacy for the Kingdom of Christ.  Our vision for Youth Convention 2015 in November is to have 1500 people in attendance .  I challenged every youth to win one person for their youth group and to invite them to Youth Convention. With the Lord’s help and your prayers, I believe we can obtain this goal.  Thank you Pastor, Youth Leader and Youth for all your support.  May the Lord continue to bless you, your family, and your ministries! 

David Guajardo
CDDC DYD
