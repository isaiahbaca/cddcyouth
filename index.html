Welcome ...


Welcome to CDDC Youth Online. CDDC Youth (previously CLAD Youth) is one of eleven Latin districts within the Assemblies Of God. Our district covers six states: Colorado, Idaho, New Mexico, Utah, Wyoming and Montana.


"The generation that the world can not change will be the generation that will change the world." We know that CDDC youth are making a difference in this world. No Doubt About It!
