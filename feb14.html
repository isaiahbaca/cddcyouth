From Our DYD:

"...The Lord your God will always be with you." Joshua 1:9.   2013 was an amazing year for CDDC youth department.   We are trusting in the Lord for a greater year in 2014.  Our vision as a youth department is for every church to have a healthy, thriving youth group in 2014. Please let me know if I can assist you and your youth leader in any way. 

The Lord is awesome!  Convention 2013 exceeded our expectations.  The floodgates of Heaven were open as the Holy Spirit submerged us with His presence among the people in attendance.  The youth came ready to receive from the Lord and left convention ready to “FINISH THE RACE”.  We know our youth are on a mission to leave a Legacy for the Kingdom of Christ.  Our vision for Youth Convention 2014 in November is to have 1500 people in attendance .  I challenged every youth to win one person for their youth group and to invite them to Youth Convention. With the Lord’s help and your prayers, I believe we can obtain this goal.  Thank you Pastor, Youth Leader and Youth for all your support.  May the Lord continue to bless you, your family, and your ministries! 

 

Fine Arts

Thank you to everyone who participated in our District Fine Arts Festival during Convention. We have very talented youth that God has enable with many different gifts. Let us pray and encourage the 51 youth that qualified for the National Fine Arts Festival that will take place in Columbus, OH. Our Youth will participate in 24 presentations represented in 10 categories during the week of July 28-31. Thank you again for all your support, please start getting ready for our District Fine Art Festival in November. If you have any questions please contact me at cindirosado@gmail.com, or Pastor David at dguajardo67@gmail.com


Cyndi Rosado-Martinez
CDDC Fine Arts Coordinator.

Speed the Light

We serve a great God! Thanks to everyone who gave to Speed the Light in 2013. Collectively, CDDC Youth gave $20,450.39 to help fund our STL projects. We were able to purchase a 12-passenger van for our own CDDC Missionary Isaac Olivarez and 125 kindles for church planters in India. Let’s continue to work hard with our youth to raise more money than ever before for STL Missions. Thank you again for your heart for missions, may we work together in 2014 and reach our goal of $100,000. If you need assistance in any way please contact me at robin_vega@gmail.com or Pastor David at dguajardo67@gmail.com.

Robin Vega
CDDC Speed the Light Coordinator
