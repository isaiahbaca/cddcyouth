CDDC Youth Convention

Please make plans to join us for Youth Convention 2015, November 25th - 27th. We have amazing speakers, bands and activities planned for Youth Convention. We are excited that the Hyatt Regency Downtown Denver will be our host hotel. Our services and activities will be in the Hyatt's phenomenal Centennial Ballroom. Please call the Hyatt Hotel Reservations (1-888-421-1442) before November 3rd to receive our special rate of $83 per night. Early registrations postmarked on or before November 3rd will be $75, which will include a t-shirt. Registrations received after November 3rd will be $85 and will not include a t-shirt.

2015 AIMs Events

Our annual summer missions trip to New Orleans was a huge success. This year was amazing! Our students had a great time during the week of the mission trip as they experienced the world of inner city missions. God did a great work in them and through them. Be sure to make plans to attend our next aim's mission trip in 2016!



CDDC Previous AIMS trip include Washington DC, Nicaragua, Panama, Denver Urban Outreach, and the L.A Dream Center.
