From Our DYD:

One of my favorite writers on the topic of Leadership is John Maxwell. Here is an excerpt from "Developing the Leader Within You" where he gives a great definition of what it is to be a leader.  

" Everyone talks about it; few understand it. Most people want it; few achieve it. There are over 50 definitions and descriptions of it in my personal files. What is this intriguing subject called 'leadership'? Perhaps because most of us want to be leaders, we become emotionally involved when trying to define leadership. Or, perhaps because we know one, we try to copy his or her behavior and describe leadership as a personality. Ask ten people to define leadership and you will probably receive ten different answers. After more than five decades of observing leadership within my family and many years of developing my own leadership potential, i have home to this conclusion; leadership is influence. Thats it. Nothing more, nothing less. My favorite leadership proverb is: He who thinketh he leadeth and hath no one following him is only taking a walk. "

As a youth leader it is important to remember to spend time with your youth to have the ability to influence them to live a Godly life. Your influence will change this generation for the Kingdom of God! 

- David Guajardo
